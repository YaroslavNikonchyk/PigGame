Instructions to the Pig-game

  1. Choose with a friend who will be player 1 and who will be player 2
  2. Roll the dice and dial the score, but keep in mind that if 1 rolls out, the score is reset to zero
  3. In the process of rolling the dice, you can press HOLD, this will save your result to CURRENT
  4. The first player, who will reach 100 points - WINS
